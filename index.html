6th STOP: THE DINER

A trip around Colorado is incomplete without a full American meal. We are stopping by a diner for some burger and strawberry milkshake. This drop is inspired by the tiles often seen on gas station diners across America. See you in our next destination!

5th STOP: THE RANCH

After a long hike and long trip down the slopes, a visit to the ranch is in order. We are living our cowgirl fantasy in our workwear pants and straw hats.

4th STOP: THE SNOW MOUNTAINS

We are heading to the world's most popular slopes in Aspen for a ski. Stay ready warm and cozy because it's a long way down the slopes!

3rd STOP: THE ROCKY MOUNTAINS

A trip to Colorado is never complete without a visit to the Rocky Mountains. As the temperature drops and we enter the rainy days, we don ourselves in warmer clothes with prints that remind us of the summer days past.

2nd STOP: THE LAKE

We drove from our motel to the lake for a picnic by plains of greens and flowers.

Revel in the last warm breezes and flower petals of the Colorado summer.

1st STOP: MOTEL STIEGLITZ

We are landing in Denver, Colorado! We are taking you on a full-on road trip around the state to see it in all its glory - plains of greens and flowers, lakes, snow mountains, you name it!

First stop: our motel. Let's get settled in before out great adventure.
